Below are all of the fire categories used in the Quiet Corner Alerts system. All categories are listed with a description as well as guidance on updates and upgrades.

1 ALARM FIRE

Use this category for any structure fire where an official 1st alarm has been requested. For working fires less than a 1st alarm use Minor Fire
Page Speed: Units on scene
Required Information: Street Address, Size up of structure, entrapment
Update Criteria: Additional alarms, major changes(ie defensive ops, evac tones, collapses, entrapment/rescues

ALARM ACTIVATION

Use this category for any Fire or Carbon Monoxide alarm activation. This category may be paged at Time of Call. If Carbon Monoxide or active fire are present prioritize the propper fire category or hazmat respectively.
Page Speed: Time of Call
Required Information: Street Address, Type of Alarm, level of response
Update Criteria: If Carbon Monoxide or active fire are present page proper fire category or hazmat respectively.
Example:

BRUSH FIRE

Use this category for any brush fire.
Page Speed: Units on scene
Required Information:Cross street, size, rate of spread(if avalable), type of fuel(if available), exposures at risk(if any), level of response
Update Criteria:Location Change, +1 Acre size update, +2 Department Response, +5 Individual unit response, 1 or more special unit response(Drone, state resources, task forces, utvs, helecopter)

HAZMAT

Use this category for any hazardous material spill or confirmed gas leak or reading. This could be in a structure, from a vehicle, or any other source
Page Speed: Units on scene
Required Information:street address(cross street acceptable if large scale incident), material spilled, amount, type of container spilled from, level of response, injuries
Update Criteria: Evacuations/ road closures, deep/task force response, unkown materials identified, injuries

INDUSTRIAL ACCIDENT

This category is for any sort of industrial injury or entrapment. This could be in a factory, farm, or other location using large machines or heavy equipment.
Page Speed: Units on scene
Required Information:Street Address(and business if available), type of injury, cause(ie dumptruck, stuck in machinery), level of response
Update Criteria: upgrade to level of response, fatality, special resources requested

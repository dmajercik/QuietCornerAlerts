About Quiet Corner Alert

Originating in the Quite Corner of Connecticut very few agencies had the staffing or resources for a Public Information Officer(PIO). This left a critical gap in the communites insight into emergencies going on around them. Quiet Corner Alerts was founded in and effort to keep the community aware or these incidents as they occur in the communities of our users. We are an independent service that is not affiliated with any municipality or agency. We rely on tips and information our amazing dispatchers gather from monitoring emergency radio frequencies.
